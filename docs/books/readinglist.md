## 9. Edition Rules

### Edition-sensitive
* **C++ Primer** – prefer 5th edition only.
* **A Tour of C++** – prefer 2nd or 3rd edition.
* **Effective Modern C++** – any normal edition is fine.
* **Professional CMake** – prefer recent editions only.
* **Linux kernel/device-driver books** – useful conceptually, but verify APIs online.

### Edition-flexible
* Algorithms / DSA textbooks
* Calculus textbooks
* Linear algebra textbooks
* Discrete math textbooks
* Classic C books
* OS concept books
* Electronics and circuits textbooks

---

## 10. Duplicate Avoidance

Do not buy more generic embedded C/C++ books unless:
* ARM Cortex-M specific
* RTOS / real-time specific
* safety-critical / testing specific
* unusually respected
* under $5
